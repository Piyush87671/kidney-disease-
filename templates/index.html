🧠 Chronic Kidney Disease Prediction System

Empower Your Kidney Health 🩺

🧪 Early Testing

Get your kidneys tested regularly if you have diabetes or high blood pressure.

💧 Stay Hydrated

Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily to help your kidneys flush toxins.

🚭 No Smoking

Quit smoking to reduce the progression of kidney disease and heart risk.

🥦 Healthy Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables & reduce salt intake to keep your kidneys functioning well.
